HEAD:

'Cookies and Cocoa' event brings festive fun to Eastford

CUTLINES: *Adam Minor photos*

EASTFORD --- The Recreation Commission hosted its annual "Cookies &
Cocoa with Santa" event on Saturday, Dec. 6, at the Eastford Elementary
School gym, providing music, ornament decoration, crafts, treats, and
yes --- cookies and cocoa. Santa and Mrs. Claus even made a special
appearance at the event, spending time to interact with kids of all
ages. Music was provided by the Babcock Cornet Band of Ashford.
