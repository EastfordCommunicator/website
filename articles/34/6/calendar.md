## January

### Thursday, Jan. 1

Eastford Public Library will be closed for New Year's Day. However,
during the month of January we invite families to enter the world of
Narnia. Please come in and take out a copy of "The Lion, the Witch and
the Wardrobe," by C.S. Lewis. We encourage everyone to participate in a
family read aloud. At the end of the month, we will be featuring the
movie at the library.

### Tuesday, Jan. 6

Eastford Public Library hosts "Homeschool Hour" at 10:30 a.m.
Please register, as space is limited. 

### Thursday, Jan. 8

Eastford Public Library hosts "Storytime" at 11 a.m. Join us for a
story, craft, snack and time of free play.  

### Saturday, Jan. 10

Eastford Public Library hosts a "Winter Wonderland" at 10:30 a.m. We
will have a craft, homemade snow, and create a sweet winter treat.
Please register.

Eastford Baptist Church will host its monthly Men's Prayer Breakfast on
Saturday, Jan. 10, at 7 a.m. All men are invited to come out for a time
of prayer, fellowship, and food. We need these times more than ever so
don't miss out on this opportunity!

### Sunday, Jan. 11

On Jan. 11, visit the Windham-Tolland 4-H Camp Open House from 1-3 p.m.
at 326 Taft Pond Rd., Pomfret. Meet the director and other camp staff,
take a camp tour, registration materials available. Take advantage of
early bird discounts -- details available at the open house or on the
website, www.4hcampct.org.

### Wednesday, Jan. 14

Eastford Public Library hosts "Maker's Night" from 6 to 7:30 p.m. for
teens and adults. Please join us!

### Thursday, Jan. 15

Eastford Public Library hosts "Storytime" at 11 a.m. Join us for a
story, craft, snack and time of free play.  

### Saturday, Jan. 17

Eastford Public Library hosts an Author Talk with gothic, horror writer
Christa Carmen at 1 p.m. Please register; space is limited.

### Thursday, Jan. 22

Eastford Public Library hosts "Storytime" at 11 a.m. Join us for a
story, craft, snack and time of free play.

### Saturday, Jan. 24

On Jan. 24, join us at Windham-Tolland 4-H Camp from 5-8 p.m., for a
fun, family "Sno-Ball" Dinner Dance. There will be a sit-down baked
chicken and mac and cheese dinner prepared by our amazing camp cook and
We-Lik-It Ice Cream Bar for dessert. We will have a DJ, dancing for all
ages, door prizes, raffles, family pictures and a great night out to
beat the winter doldrums. All proceeds benefit the Jim Logee Campership
Fund. Adults $25, Kids 10 and under $15. Buy tickets by Jan. 20 by
calling the camp at 860-974-3379. Snow date is Jan. 31.

### Thursday, Jan 29

Eastford Public Library hosts "Storytime" at 11 a.m. Join us for a
story, craft, snack and time of free play.

### Friday, Jan. 30

Eastford Public Library hosts a "Movie Night" at 6 p.m. featuring "The
Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe." Snacks and
refreshments will be provided. Please register.
