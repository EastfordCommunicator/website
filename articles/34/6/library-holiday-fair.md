---
title: "Holiday Fair Another 'Resounding Success' at the Library"
---

EASTFORD --- The Friends of the Eastford Public Library hosted its 2nd
Annual Holiday Fair on Saturday, Dec. 6. Festivities included a silent
auction, hand-crafted gifts, baked goods and more. All proceeds from the
Holiday Fair went to support library programs. According to Librarian
Katie Ball, the Holiday Fair was a "resounding success."
