---
title: CCE Christmas Pageant Delights
---
EASTFORD --- On Sunday, Dec. 21, the Congregational Church of Eastford
held its annual Christmas Pageant during their worship service at
Eastford Elementary School. The program celebrated the birth of Jesus
Christ four days before Christmas.
