---
title: From the Grove Cemetery Association Board of Trustees
---

- Flags: American flags and fire department flags will be removed on
Wednesday, Nov. 12. Plot owners who remove their military flag holders
for the winter are reminded to reset them the week prior to Memorial
Day.

- Trustees Meeting: Thursday, Nov. 13, 7 p.m., Ivy Glenn

- Seasonal Decorations: Mowing and turf maintenance continues well into
the month of November. If you wish to place seasonal/artificial
decorations on your plot, please wait until Thanksgiving, Nov. 27. After
March 15, 2026, the decorations will be removed for the start of the
mowing season.

- Perpetual Care Reminder: flowers that are planted at the base of a
monument must be within 12 inches of the base. If the plants are not
maintained and grow beyond that limit, they will be cut back.

- Plot/Space Purchase: Appointments can be made to view the cemetery
grounds and to select a plot for purchase. To make an appointment, call
860-428-0280.

The Eastford Grove Cemetery Association greatly appreciates the
following donors who have contributed to the beautification of the
grounds: Jim & Marian Slye; In memory of William & Helena Nieminen;
Carolyn Hazard; Lauren Anderson. Donations are tax-deductible and can be
mailed to EGCA, Inc., P.O. Box 284, Eastford, CT 06242.
