## November

### Saturday, Nov. 1

Eastford Public Library hosts their "Annual November Food Drive" for the
Eastford Food Pantry to support neighbors in need. Please bring your
non-perishable items in during library hours.

### Thursday, Nov. 6

Eastford Public Library hosts "Storytime" at 11 a.m. Join us for a
story, craft, snack and time of free play. 

### Saturday, Nov. 8

A Multi-Town Veterans Day Ceremony will be held Saturday, Nov. 8, at 11
a.m. Ashford, Eastford, Mansfield, Union, and Willington will be
celebrating together. This ceremony will be held in Mansfield at E.O.
Smith High School Auditorium, 1235 Storrs Road, Storrs. We will be
honoring all who served. This year we will have a special recognition
for the Vietnam veterans still with us. Reception to follow with light
refreshments in the cafeteria from noon to 1 p.m.

Eastford Public Library hosts "BBC Book Club" at 1 p.m. We will be
discussing "Mad Honey" by Jodi Picoult. The book will be on display and
available to check out.

### Sunday, Nov. 9

The Congregational Church of Eastford offers a seminar to help people
who face sorrow and loss as they go through the holiday season. Jesus
said, "Blessed are those who mourn for they shall be comforted." If you
have lost a loved one and face grief and sorrow this season, I invite
you to attend GriefShare "Surviving the Holidays" on Sunday, Nov. 9,
from 5:30-7:30 p.m. This seminar is designed to bring comfort, strength
and hope. This seminar will be held at our office located next to the
Post Office (4 County Rd., Eastford).

### Wednesday, Nov. 12

Eastford Public Library hosts a "Harvest Maker's Night" from 6 to 7:30
p.m. Come create with us! Please note this program takes place on the
second Wednesday of every month.

### Thursday, Nov. 13

Eastford Public Library hosts "Storytime" at 11 a.m. Join us for a
story, craft, snack and time of free play. 

### Friday, Nov. 14

Eastford Public Library hosts a "Stuffed Animal Sleepover" at 6 p.m.
Please join us for a Pajama Storytime before we tuck in our bravest
stuffed animals to sleepover at the library. After a good night's sleep
come pick them up on Saturday morning!

### Sunday, Nov. 16

The Congregational Church of Eastford offers a seminar to help people
who face sorrow and loss as they go through the holiday season. Jesus
quoted a prophet who said, "the LORD ... has sent me to bind up the
brokenhearted." If divorce has upended your household and holiday
expectations, you are invited to attend DivorceCare "Surviving the
Holidays" on Sunday, Nov. 16 from 5:30 to 7:30 p.m. Here you will find
comfort and practical help for social events, family gatherings and hope
for the future. This seminar will be held at our office located next to
the Post Office (4 County Rd., Eastford).

### Thursday, Nov. 20

Eastford Public Library hosts "Storytime" at 11 a.m. Join us for a
story, craft, snack and time of free play. At 5 p.m. the Friends of the
Eastford Public Library will have their monthly meeting. All are welcome
to attend. From 6 to 7:30 p.m. we'll also be hosting a "Holiday
Workshop" to create a few items for the Holiday Fair. Anyone is welcome
to join!

### Saturday, Nov. 22

Eastford Public Library hosts a "Thanksgiving Craft" for children at
10:30 a.m. Please register for this event.

### Sunday, Nov. 23

You are invited to Eastford Baptist Church on Sunday, Nov. 23, for our
annual Thanksgiving Dinner. The dinner, which will include all the
traditional Thanksgiving menu items, will be held directly after the
Sunday Worship Service, which will begin at 10:30 a.m. Join us for a
time of fellowship and fun, as well as a time of sharing of what you are
thankful for.

### Wednesday, Nov. 26

Eastford Public Library will be closing at 2 p.m. We will be open from
10 a.m. to 2 p.m. Please come in and grab your holiday reads! We will
resume normal hours on Saturday.
