HEAD:

Trunk or treat event an annual favorite at Buell's Orchard

CUTLINES: *Adam Minor photos*

EASTFORD --- Buell's Orchard held its annual Trunk or Treat event on
Sunday, Oct. 26. Hundreds from all around the area showed up to more
than a dozen trunks of various themes to hunt for treats and enjoy an
afternoon of dress-up fun.
