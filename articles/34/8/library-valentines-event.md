---
title: Valentine's Day Craft a Sweet Treat at the Library
---

EASTFORD --- What could be sweeter than a handmade Valentine? Young
patrons of the Eastford Public Library gathered on Valentine's Day,
Saturday, Feb. 14, to make Valentine's Day cards and decorate
heart-shaped sugar cookies.
